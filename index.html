My Journey 🌿

I grew up in a humble environment in Kampala, where life often felt overwhelming and uncertain.

There were moments where I felt lost, anxious, and unsure about my path. But through those experiences, I began a journey of self-discovery, healing, and growth.

Today, I am building Peace & Purpose—a platform dedicated to mental health awareness, personal growth, and helping young people find meaning in their lives.

My mission is to create a space where people feel seen, heard, and empowered to become their best selves.
