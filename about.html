About Me 🌿

I grew up in a humble environment in one of the largest communities in Kampala, where life often felt overwhelming and uncertain.

Through my journey, I have experienced moments of feeling lost, anxious, and unsure—but also moments of growth, healing, and self-discovery.

Today, I am building Peace & Purpose—a platform dedicated to mental health awareness, personal growth, and helping young people find meaning in their lives.

My mission is simple: to create a safe space where people feel seen, heard, and inspired to become their best selves.
